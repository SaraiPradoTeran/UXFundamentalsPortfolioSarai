Project Jobs-To-Be-Done with Children

Client: Educational Site

About the Client
It is a website followed by more than 16 million Mexican children to get help with their homework.
Objective
Identify if its tools and content help in academic progress; investigate and define the jobs they do (Jobs to be done - JTBD) for their users; suggest how they can convert from non-profit to a monetizing business, based on JTBD.
Methodology & Tools
  • Remote usability testing (via Zoom) moderated in pairs.
  • Semi structured interviews.
  • Jobs To Be Done by Jim Kalbach.
  • Heuristics (various authors); cognitive psychology and Gestalt.
  • Value proposition canvas.
  • Persona canvas.
Summary
In this study, 36 usability tests and interviews were carried out where 2 moderators inquired with 22 children, 5 teachers and 9 parents, about the moment to study and do the homework to find the JTBD of each of the profiles. In addition, tasks were defined to evaluate the usability of certain sections of the platform. Based on the findings, suggestions for improving the interface and their business model are made.

My Role
The team carried out the project, and my individual contribution was in the following activities:
  • Recruitment of participants.
  • Creation of the moderator guide.
  • Moderation and observation of usability tests.
  • Analysis and formulation, and categorization of JTBD.
  • Preparation of report and presentation of results.
Challenges
  • Recruit the appropriate participant profile considering age and technological restrictions.
  • Create moderator guides and moderate usability tests with children and pre-teens.
  • Define a value proposition based on the JTBD of 3 different profiles (children, teachers and parents).
Results
This study is a work in progress, however, at the end of the current phase, archetypes and personas were defined based on the JTBD; usability and user experience problems were detected and solutions were proposed, and a value proposition was established according to the business and users objectives.
